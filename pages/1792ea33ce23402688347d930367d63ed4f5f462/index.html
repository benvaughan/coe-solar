The Idea

Organic solar modules hold the possibility of being able to generate low-cost electricity, on almost any surface.

Printed

Solar modules are printed using roll-to-roll (R2R) technology, the same way stickers, labels, and other printed products are produced.

Lightweight

1000 square metres of organic solar is a little heavier than an adult male (100 kg). Compare that to traditional silicon solar, which weighs the same as 3 large african elephants for the same area (11 tons)!

Low cost

Organic solar modules are almost entirely plastic, meaning their cost is extremely low.

Flexible

The modules arrive rolled up on cores, similar to paper towel. Able to be wrapped around almost any surface, their flexibility opens up almost endless possibilities for deployment.

Recyclable

As the modules are almost entirely plastic, they are completely recylable.

Moonlight

Finally, low light performance is possible with solar! This one's for you Paul ;-P
